About the architect

Chetan Kumar

Digital Tool Architect

Chetan designs and maintains small, fast, honest web tools. CalculateIt grew out of a frustration with bloated calculator sites — pop-ups, trackers, and ten ads before a single number.

Every calculator here is written by hand, tested against published state DMV fee schedules, and shipped without surveillance scripts. The result is a quiet corner of the internet that respects your time.

If you want to talk shop, share a bug, or suggest a new calculator, the door is open.
